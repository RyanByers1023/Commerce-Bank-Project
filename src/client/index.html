Who made this?

We are Team Last Minute, a group of four members: Logan, Hayley, Ryan, and Ibraham. This simulator is our software engineering capstone project, developed in collaboration between Commerce Bank and The University of Missouri-Kansas City.

Even as a college project, our goal is to provide a meaningful learning experience that helps you understand trading mechanics and market dynamics in a risk-free environment.
