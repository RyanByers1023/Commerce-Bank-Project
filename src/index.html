Who made this?

We are Team Last Minute. We have four members: Logan, Hayley, Ryan, and Ibraham. This is our software engineering capstone project; this project's guidelines were provided via a collaboration between Commerce Bank and The University of Missouri-Kansas City.

Even with this being a college project, our hope still is that you are able to extract some meaningful trading information from your time with our simulator!
