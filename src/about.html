About The Project

Learn to trade without the risk. Our simulator provides a realistic stock market experience for both beginners and experienced traders.

Our Mission

The Project Last Minute Stock Market Simulator was created to help users understand how the stock market works without risking real money. Whether you're a student, a curious beginner, or someone looking to practice new trading strategies, our platform provides a safe environment to learn and experiment.

Our simulator mimics real market conditions with:

  • Real-time price fluctuations based on simulated market movements
  • News events that impact stock performance
  • Portfolio management tools to track your investments
  • Multiple timeframes to analyze stock performance

Start with $10,000 in virtual cash and build your portfolio through strategic buying and selling. Track your progress and see how your investment decisions perform over time.
